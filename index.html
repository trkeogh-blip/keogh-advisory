Scope of Engagements
How We Engage

Keogh Advisory Group accepts engagements where operational complexity, regulatory exposure, or stakeholder dynamics require experienced, independent leadership. The firm operates across three related service lines, each grounded in direct operating experience rather than advisory distance.

01
Interim Executive & Operational Advisory
Interim CEO and COO engagements, operational assessments, and advisory support for regulated businesses navigating leadership transitions, performance challenges, or complex stakeholder environments. Available for project-based deployment or sustained interim roles.
Engagements may be structured on behalf of a lender, board, investor group, or directly with the operating company. All engagements are principal-led.
02
Asset Management for Licensed & Previously Licensed Facilities
Physical oversight, systems management, regulatory continuity, and government affairs for cannabis facilities that are vacant, transitioning ownership, or in disposition. This is not general property management. It requires direct familiarity with CCC license continuity, Host Community Agreements, provisional license renewals, and municipal relationships — and the ability to maintain those while keeping carrying costs minimized.
Keogh Advisory Group can serve as the operational point of contact for lenders, receivers, or owners managing licensed assets through a transition period — preserving license value and regulatory standing while disposition or ownership transfer proceeds.
03
Fiduciary & Special Situations
Independent fiduciary oversight, operational stabilization, regulatory compliance, stakeholder management, and asset preservation for businesses navigating complex transitions, lender-directed situations, or stakeholder-driven environments.
Engagements are evaluated on a matter-by-matter basis. Conflicts are assessed prior to any substantive engagement.

Keogh Advisory Group is a Massachusetts-based firm led by Timothy Keogh, an entrepreneur, CEO, and operator with more than two decades of experience building, operating, and advising businesses in regulated and capital-intensive industries, including cannabis cultivation, manufacturing and retail operations, real estate development, marina and waterfront development, and marine operations.

Tim has built and led companies, raised capital through debt and equity markets, and managed complex stakeholder relationships with lenders, investors, regulators, and municipalities. His operating background includes licensed cannabis cultivation, manufacturing and retail operations, cannabis licensing and regulatory compliance under the Massachusetts Cannabis Control Commission, public company leadership, facility development, optimization of building operations, marina and waterfront operations, and direct engagement on lender workouts, distressed assets, and complex stakeholder situations.

He has run businesses — not just advised them.

Unlike many advisors, receivers, and restructuring professionals, Tim's experience comes from direct responsibility for operations, capital formation, regulatory compliance, development, and stakeholder management.

Every engagement is principal-led. Tim Keogh is directly involved in every matter.
