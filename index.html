About

Operationally
Grounded.

Keogh Advisory Group is a Massachusetts-based consulting firm founded and led by Timothy Keogh, a founder, CEO, and operator with more than 20 years of experience across regulated industries, real estate, and marine and waterfront operations.

Tim has built and led companies from the ground up, raised capital through debt and equity markets, and managed complex stakeholder relationships with lenders, investors, regulators, and municipalities. His operating background spans facility development and construction, licensing and compliance in regulated industries, and direct engagement on lender workouts and distressed asset situations on behalf of banks and financial institutions.

He has run businesses — not just advised them. That distinction shapes how every engagement is structured and delivered.

Every engagement is principal-led. Tim Keogh is directly involved in every matter.
